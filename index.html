Crystal Collector

You will be given a random target score at the start of the game

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to target score, you lose if your total score goes above the target score.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts the values of each crystal will change.
